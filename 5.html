Indigo's proprietary Crop Health index(CHI) uses daily satellite imagery to assess the the greenness or chlorophyll content an density of a crop's canopy. Intensity of canopy greenness correlates strongly with overall crop biomass and yield.

Crop health anomalies indicate a positive or negative deviations in canopy health from the 10-year average.
